Yes. Zoho Books is free for businesses under certain revenue thresholds. Even for larger businesses, plans start at just $15/month‚Äîa fraction of QuickBooks. It includes invoicing, expenses, bank reconciliation, and all the features construction companies need.

What exactly syncs between SiteProc and Zoho Books?

Expenses (with receipt photos), invoices, payments, vendors, and project allocations. The sync is two-way, so changes in either system are reflected in both.

How does the sync work technically?

It's automatic. When you approve an expense in SiteProc, it appears in Zoho Books within minutes. No manual export/import, no CSV files, no double-entry.

Absolutely. Every expense and invoice can be allocated to a specific project. SiteProc shows real-time budget vs. actual, and Zoho Books provides the financial reports you need for taxes and analysis.

What about payment applications for commercial projects?

Will my accountant know how to use Zoho Books?
