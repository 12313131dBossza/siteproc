FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SiteProc

What is SiteProc and who is it for? +

SiteProc is a comprehensive construction management platform designed specifically for the U.S. construction industry. It's perfect for construction companies, contractors, project managers, site supervisors, and material suppliers who need to manage projects, track orders, coordinate deliveries, and monitor expenses all in one place.

How long does implementation take? +

Most companies are up and running within 1-2 weeks. We provide comprehensive onboarding support including data migration, team training, and custom configuration. Our dedicated implementation team works with you to ensure a smooth transition from your current system with minimal disruption to your operations.

Can I integrate SiteProc with my existing tools? +

Yes! SiteProc offers a robust REST API with 50+ endpoints for seamless integration with your existing tools. We support integrations with popular accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero), project management tools, and custom internal systems. Our team can help set up custom integrations specific to your workflow.

Is my data secure? +

Absolutely. We take security seriously. SiteProc is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure with SOC 2 compliance, end-to-end encryption, and company-based data isolation. We implement role-based access control with 6 permission levels, maintain regular security audits, and offer 99.9% uptime SLA. Your data is backed up daily and stored in secure, geographically distributed data centers.

Do you offer mobile access? +

Yes! SiteProc is fully responsive and works seamlessly on mobile devices. Site managers can receive deliveries, upload proof of delivery documents, update project progress, and manage orders right from their smartphones or tablets. The mobile experience is optimized for on-site use with offline capabilities for areas with poor connectivity.

What kind of support do you provide? +

We provide comprehensive support including email support, live chat, and phone support for enterprise customers. You'll also get access to detailed documentation, video tutorials, and a dedicated customer success manager. Our support team is available during business hours with extended hours for critical issues. We also offer on-site training for larger teams.

Can I try SiteProc before committing? +

Yes! We offer a personalized demo and trial period so you can test SiteProc with your actual projects and workflows. Contact our sales team to schedule a demo where we'll walk you through the platform, answer your specific questions, and set up a trial environment configured for your needs.

How does pricing work? +

We offer custom enterprise pricing based on your company size, number of projects, and specific feature requirements. This ensures you only pay for what you need. Pricing includes unlimited users, full platform access, dedicated support, and regular updates. Contact our sales team for a personalized quote tailored to your business.

What if I need custom features? +

We understand that every construction company has unique needs. Our enterprise plan includes the option for custom feature development. Our development team will work with you to understand your requirements and build custom modules or integrations that fit perfectly into your workflow. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
